2. Data We Collect

Tourneo does not require an account and does not include a login. Tournaments, players, and other content you create are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or otherwise have access to this content.

The only personal data we process is limited technical data, collected automatically through analytics and crash-reporting tools so that we can keep the Application stable and functional:

  • Device and technical information – device model, operating system and version, application version, language, and region.
  • Identifiers – a pseudonymous identifier assigned to your installation of the Application by our analytics tool.
  • IP address – processed by our service providers, primarily for approximate location and security purposes.
  • Usage data – in-app events and interactions, such as which screens are opened and which features are used.
  • Diagnostic data – crash reports and error logs generated when the Application encounters a problem.

We do not knowingly collect the names or other details of players you enter into the Application. This information remains on your device.

3. Purposes and Legal Basis of Processing

We process this technical data for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the security and stability of the Application – crash and error reporting, based on our legitimate interest in providing a functioning and secure service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • To understand how the Application is used and to improve it – analytics, based on your consent where required, which you may withdraw at any time (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

We do not use your data for marketing, and we do not carry out automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning you.

4. Data Retention

We retain technical data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above:

  • Analytics data – up to 12 months.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs – up to 12 months.

These periods reflect the configuration of our service providers and may be shortened over time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited technical data with the following service providers, which act as our processors under data processing agreements:

  • PostHog – product analytics.
  • Sentry – crash and error reporting.

We may also disclose data to public authorities where required by applicable law.

6. International Data Transfers

We use the European data regions of our service providers, so your technical data is processed within the European Economic Area (EEA) wherever possible. Where any transfer outside the EEA occurs – for example because a provider is established in the United States – it is protected by appropriate safeguards, such as:

  • the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs); and/or
  • certification under the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework, where applicable.

7. Your Rights under GDPR

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)

  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@tourneo.app. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO) in Poland, or with your local supervisory authority.
